Enter Text
Instructions: Enter the entire text that you would like to memorize then click "Start."

Practice
Instructions: Type the word in the box and press enter. If you enter the correct value, then you will automatically be moved to the next word. If you have not entered the correct value, you will remain at the current word.

At any time you can press the "Hint" button to reveal the next letter, or the "Reveal" button to reveal the entire word. You can utilize the check box to the left of the word to make a note of any words you had trouble with.

When you are finished, press the "Reset" button to start over.
